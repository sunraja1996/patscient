Ms. Padma Sree

Founder

Patent Agent

Padma Sree G, is an experienced registered Patent Agent with over 10 years of on-field expertise in handling patent matters. She holds a strong academic foundation, including an M. Tech in Biotechnology from IIT Madras and an M.Sc. in Biochemistry along with a PG Diploma in Patent Law from NALSAR, Hyderabad.

A registered patent agent (IN/PA 3408) specializes in various aspects of intellectual property with specialization in patents, she is well versed with Patent Searches, Patent Specification Drafting, prosecution and Filings. She has spent a year and half as a researcher contributing to an internationally recognized publication in the Journal of Pharmacology and Toxicology, her experience in both scientific and patent fields enable her to provide the inventors with strategic insights and effective solutions tailored to their needs.

Ms Divya Sridharan

Patent Agent

Divya Sridharan is a seasoned professional with 14 years of extensive experience in Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) and a proven track record as a Senior Research Analyst. She specializes in IP research, market analysis, and competitive intelligence, leveraging her expertise to provide actionable insights and strategic recommendations.

She holds a P.G. Diploma in Patents Law from NALSAR University, Hyderabad, and an M.Sc. in Biotechnology from SRM University, Chennai. Since 2016, Divya has honed her skills in patent drafting, prosecution, and filing, contributing to numerous successful patent applications. Her background also includes publication research and effective client interactions, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of client needs and industry trends.

In addition to her analytical work, Divya served as a Visiting Faculty at UPES, Dehradun in 2021, where she shares her knowledge and insights with aspiring professionals. She is a Registered Patent Agent and brings a deep understanding of the Indian patent landscape.
